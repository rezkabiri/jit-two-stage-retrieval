# Project Phoenix: Strategic Acquisition Proposal

**Date**: March 2025
**Author**: M&A Strategy Team
**Classification**: STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL - EXECUTIVE ACCESS ONLY

## Executive Overview
Proposed acquisition of **NeoBank EU**, a leading digital bank in the Eurozone.

## Strategic Rationale
1. **Market Entry**: Instant access to 5 million EU-based retail customers.
2. **Technology**: NeoBank's proprietary cloud-native ledger system will replace our aging core banking infrastructure.
3. **Synergies**: Estimated cost savings of $250M annually by year three.

## Valuation & Deal Structure
- **Offer Price**: $2.4 Billion (35% premium over NeoBank's last funding round).
- **Structure**: 60% Cash, 40% Stock.
- **Timeline**: Initial approach in April; intended closing by Q4 2025.

## Known Risks
- **Regulatory Hurdles**: Likely to face intense scrutiny from the European Central Bank (ECB).
- **Integration**: Significant risk in migrating NeoBank's agile customer base to our compliance-heavy systems.
