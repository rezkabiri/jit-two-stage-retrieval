# 2025 Global Economic Outlook

**Date**: January 2025
**Author**: Global Investment Strategy Team
**Classification**: PUBLIC

## Overview
The 2025 global economy is characterized by a "Grand Reset." After several years of volatility, central banks have largely achieved their inflation targets, leading to a period of normalized interest rates.

## Key Themes
1. **GDP Growth**: Global GDP is expected to grow by 3.2% in 2025, driven by resilience in emerging markets.
2. **Inflation**: Inflation has stabilized at around 2.0% across major economies (US, EU, UK).
3. **Equities**: We remain cautiously optimistic on equities, particularly in the technology and healthcare sectors.

## Summary for Retail Investors
2025 presents a favorable environment for long-term diversified portfolios. While geopolitical risks remain, the fundamental economic data suggests a "soft landing" has been achieved.
