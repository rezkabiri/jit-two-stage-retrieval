# 2025 Internal Market Risk Assessment

**Date**: February 2025
**Author**: Enterprise Risk Management (ERM)
**Classification**: INTERNAL - FINANCE USE ONLY

## Confidential Summary
This report analyzes potential "tail risks" for our 2025 portfolio. While the public outlook is positive, our proprietary models indicate specific areas of vulnerability.

## Critical Vulnerabilities
1. **Interest Rate Swap Exposure**: Our $50B interest rate swap portfolio is sensitive to a secondary inflation spike, which could lead to a $400M mark-to-market loss if the Fed reverses course.
2. **Emerging Market Debt**: Increased exposure to debt in Southeast Asian markets (specifically Indonesia and Vietnam) is high. Default risks have risen to 4.5% in our "Worst Case" scenario.
3. **Liquidity Squeeze**: In the event of a geopolitical crisis, our HQLA (High-Quality Liquid Assets) levels might drop below the required 120% LCR (Liquidity Coverage Ratio).

## Remediation Strategy
- **Hedging**: Increase inflation-protected security holdings by 15% before Q2.
- **De-risking**: Reduce exposure to unrated emerging market corporate debt.
